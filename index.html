My 5 dream vacation zones

Machu Picchu, Peru

A UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the most famous ancient ruins in the world, Machu Picchu offers incredible history and breathtaking views.

Things to Do

  • Trek the Inca Trail to reach Machu Picchu.
  • Visit the Sun Gate for panoramic views of the ancient city.
  • Explore the Sacred Valley and its ruins.
  • Learn about the fascinating history of the Incas at the site.
  • Take a scenic train ride through the Sacred Valley.

Tokyo, Japan

A bustling metropolis where ancient traditions meet futuristic technology, Tokyo offers something for everyone, from historic temples to vibrant nightlife.

Things you can do

  • Visit the Meiji Shrine, one of Tokyo's most important Shinto shrines.
  • Wander through the busy streets of Shibuya and cross the famous Shibuya Crossing.
  • Take a day trip to Mount Fuji for hiking or sightseeing.
  • Explore the tranquil gardens of the Imperial Palace.
  • Shop in Harajuku for unique fashion and street culture.

Dubai, UAE

A city known for luxury, skyscrapers, desert adventures, and vibrant nightlife. Dubai is an ultra-modern oasis in the heart of the desert.

Things to Do

  • Visit the Burj Khalifa, the tallest building in the world.
  • Go dune bashing and sandboarding in the Dubai Desert.
  • Explore the Dubai Mall for shopping and entertainment.
  • Take a luxury yacht tour along the coastline.
  • Experience traditional culture at the Dubai Museum and the Gold Souk.

Great Barrier Reef, Australia

The world's largest coral reef system, offering world-class diving and snorkeling in one of the most biodiverse marine environments.

Things you can do

  • Snorkel or dive in the crystal-clear waters to see vibrant coral and marine life.
  • Visit the Whitsunday Islands for stunning beaches.
  • Explore the Daintree Rainforest, the oldest tropical rainforest in the world.
  • Take a scenic flight over the reef for a bird’s-eye view.
  • Relax on Whitehaven Beach, known for its white silica sand.

Iceland

Known for its surreal landscapes, Iceland boasts waterfalls, volcanoes, hot springs, and the chance to see the Northern Lights.

Things to Do

  • Visit the Blue Lagoon for a relaxing geothermal spa experience.
  • Hike to see the stunning Gullfoss waterfall.
  • Explore the black sand beaches of Vik.
  • Take a dip in the Secret Lagoon or relax in natural hot springs.
  • Try to catch the Northern Lights in winter.
